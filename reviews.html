Reviews

Reviews of games that I have played and recommendations.

Wyrmspan

Wyrmspan is a great game for both people who have played Wingspan and those who are new to those games.

Flamecraft

Flamecraft is an adorable game where you get to work alongside cute dragons in a farmer's market. Despite the premise, it has a lot of complexity and will be a great addition to anyone's game cupboard.
