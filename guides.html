Guides

Skull

Skull is a game of bluffing and deception. Each player will lay down tiles and then place bets on how many tiles they think they can flip over before hitting a skull. Caution is advised, as you never know who has placed their skull tile in what spot in their pile.

Munchkin

Munchkin is the classic game of kicking doors, looting monstsers, and stabbing friends. As you play through this game, you will find weapons and armor to upgrade your character, defeating bigger and badder monsters up until you reach the coveted level 10.
