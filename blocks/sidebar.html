Dead Cells: The Rogue-Lite Board Game

A board game inspired by the popular video game of its namesake, Dead Cells: The Rogue-Lite Board Game will attempt to give players that rogue-lite feeling in a board game. From exploration to mutation, this game promises a lot.

Release Date: Late 2024

Super Boss Monster

Both an expansion and stand-alone game within the Boss Monster series, this game will bring a host of new possibilities to your table. The Town Board will be a new way to find rooms to add to your dungeon.

Release Date: March 2025

Brink

A sci-fi board game set to expand the world of the Moonrakers, this resource management game aims to bring together negotiation and traditional strategies of a worker placement game.

Release Date: 2025
